Introducing the Mushroom Ottoman

These comfortable classic mushroom ottomans will be the fungi of many a hangout or stay-ins! Each one of a kind,handmade using recycled materials. We’ve got a classic mushroom style ottoman and a modern toadstool style ottoman available.

psychedelic gillies!
